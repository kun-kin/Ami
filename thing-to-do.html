WHAT WE OFFER AT HOMESTAY
People coming for holidays are from cities and come to relax and enjoy in this Hotel in Mashobra. We believe one can only feel relaxed when your mind is at peace.

Relish the beauty
of Bonfire

Sitting around roaring fire in the evening is the best way to spend an evening during your holiday. Bonfire nights are the best nights in winter and summer.
Bonfire Evening
  • Warm your body and soul around a bonfire
  • Sing, dance and celebrate with the friends and family
  • Enjoy a glass of cheer with starters
  • Enjoy a glass of cheer with starters

Outdoor BBQ

How about throwing a BBQ party for your family? Worried about the menu? Rio Homestay has an abundance of vegetables like mushroom, corn, capsicum, brinjal and many others that are good for your BBQ. Meat? There are many shops that sell frozen meats, spices and charcoal to help you throw a great BBQ party.
