some word about us
Ami Homestay is located in seclusion amidst three hills in Coorg in the State of Karnataka.
Escape from the hustle and hassle of your everyday routine and come away to the Ami Homestay, where a serene world awaits you. Throw off all your worries and slip into the scenic, relaxed atmosphere and be pampered and rejuvenated. Lay back and enjoy the beautiful perennial stream that caresses the landscape between the villas, cottages and tents and let yourself be taken to a paradise surrounded by The Hills.
